Music

Ryan has played trumpet in various groups over the past few years. Ryan started music and trumpet back in elementary school and now plays in various groups at the University of Iowa. He has played in High School Show Choir band, a Ska band, concert bands, orchestras, a brass group, marching band, and more recently in jazz bands. At the University of Iowa, Ryan obtained a music minor with a jazz emphasis. He has played in multiple jazz combos, the Latin Jazz Ensemble, and currently plays in the Johnson County Landmark Orchestra. Checkout videos and photos from his musical endeavors below!
